## 🖼️ LOGO USAGE

### Sizes

**Login Page:**
- Width: 120px
- Position: Center top
- Spacing: 15px margin bottom

**Navigation Bar:**
- Width: 45px
- Height: 45px
- Position: Top left
- Spacing: 10px margin right

**Print Headers:**
- Width: 80px
- Height: Auto
- Position: Header left
- Spacing: 15px margin right

### Clear Space
Minimum clear space around logo: 20px all sides

### Don't:
❌ Stretch or distort
❌ Change colors
❌ Add effects or shadows
❌ Place on busy backgrounds

### Do:
✅ Maintain aspect ratio
✅ Use on white or navy background
✅ Ensure readability
✅ Follow size guidelines
